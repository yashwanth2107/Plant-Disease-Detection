About

Issue Overview

The main issues of plant disease are their threat to global ecosystem,food security and economy.They are particularly challenging due to their ability to re-emerge,geographic expansions and climate changes.

Our Mission

The main mission of a plant disease detection website is to use technology to identify plant diseases from images. It aims to make this process easy for everyone, and often provides information on how to manage the detected diseases.

WHAT WE DO
  • User uploads a plant image.
  • The image is processed and segmented.
  • Features like color and shape are extracted from the image.
